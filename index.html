About ESPSA

Connect with ESPSA to enhance your academic experience and innovate in the pharmaceutical field. Stay tuned for more exciting opportunities!

Our Aim

To enrich the student experience through scientific events, professional development, and community engagement. We aim to bridge the gap between academia and practice by offering impactful research opportunities, inspiring guest talks, medical convoys, internships, creative competitions, and practical application of your knowledge, fostering a culture of excellence, collaboration, and meaningful change within the pharmacy community at E-JUST.

Our Vision

We focus on enriching the academic journey of pharmacy students at E-JUST University with a spirit of fun and creativity, providing a refreshing break from faculty stress. To empower students with new skills and meaningful experiences through engaging, inspiring events, innovative opportunities, internships, and activities while shaping bold thinkers, skilled researchers, and responsible leaders ready to make their way beyond the lecture hall.

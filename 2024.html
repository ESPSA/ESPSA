ESPSA (Research Rally Competition) 2024

Overview

The ESPSA Scientific Relay is our flagship annual competition that brings together university students to solve scientific challenges in an exciting relay format. Participants will work as a team to complete different challenges that span various disciplines in the pharmaceutical and scientific fields.

Challenges

Challenge 1: Unveiling the Invisible Threat

Explore the impact of microplastics on our ecosystem and health.
Challenge 2: Pharmacovigilance

Pharmacovigilance ensures the safety of medicines in healthcare. Raise awareness and educate others about its importance in patient care.
Challenge 3: Unlocking the Power of Biomass Wastes

Explore how biomass waste can be used as a sustainable energy source and help pave the way for a cleaner future.
Challenge 4: Tackling Vaccine Hesitancy

Vaccine hesitancy is a threat to public health. Spread awareness, dispel myths, and support vaccination as a key public health measure.

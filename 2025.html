2025 Challenge Areas

Telemedicine for Underserved Areas

How can telemedicine strategies improve delivery of temperature-sensitive medicines to remote regions lacking proper healthcare infrastructure?

Key Focus Areas:
Target populations with limited healthcare access
Overcome technology barriers
Develop step-by-step implementation plan
Ensure financial sustainability
Maintain medication viability during transport
Create affordable pricing structure
Establish stakeholder communication
Guarantee accessibility for vulnerable groups

Green Pharma Implementation

How can pharmaceutical companies adopt sustainable "Green Pharma" practices without compromising drug quality or affordability?

Key Focus Areas:
Set measurable sustainability KPIs
Develop eco-friendly manufacturing
Implement sustainable packaging
Ensure regulatory compliance
Maintain cost-effectiveness
Preserve product efficacy
Create scalable global solutions
Optimize green supply chain

Pharmacy Emergency Preparedness

How can pharmacies develop robust emergency systems to maintain medication access during disasters or supply chain disruptions?

Key Focus Areas:
Prioritize patient safety protocols
Build resilient supply chains
Create adaptable crisis response plans
Improve coordination systems
Design implementable local solutions
Ensure regulatory compliance
Develop rapid response mechanisms
Address medication scarcity issues

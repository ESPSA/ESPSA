Under the supervision of the PharmD program at E-JUST, an impactful medical convoy was held in Al-Ula Village, Borg Al-Arab, for complete assistance in providing free-of-cost medications among villagers. Medical convoys are one of the most important modalities for bringing healthcare to the doorsteps of people in far-flung areas and thus narrowing the gap between providers of healthcare and the populace.

This is a great step undertaken by the PharmD at E-JUST to bring in positive changes in health and well-being among villagers. Such initiatives make available the needed medical facilities and showcase community outreach and service in the stream of pharmacy. It is through this direct involvement with the community that the program shows how deeply committed it is to fostering public health and empowering people through accessible healthcare solutions.

Date: 10-06-2024

Venue: The village of Al-Ula (Borg Al-Arab)

Parties who donated medicines:

  • EVA Pharm.
  • Amriya for Pharmaceutical Industries S.A.E
  • MUP Pharm.
  • PharmD program.

Participant Doctors (specialties):

  • Pediatric
  • Internal medicine
  • Obstetrics and gynaecology

The number of villagers who have benefited from medical care: Between 500-1000

ESPSA 2024: Research Rally Competition

ESPSA at E-JUST is proud to announce its first-ever Research Rally Competition 2024-ESPSA, considered one of the most interesting annual events. It is a competition between teams of different universities presenting their innovative projects in solving major scientific challenges.

During this first event, four winning teams emerged after presenting their projects in front of a panel of expert evaluators. Each team dealt with one of the big four challenges set for the competition:

  • Pharmacovigilance: Ensuring the safety and efficiency of medicines, monitoring, and reporting any kind of adverse reaction to drugs.
  • Microplastics: To study the effects of microplastics on ecosystems and human health, besides mitigating measures.
  • Vaccine Hesitancy: The development of effective ways of surmounting the problem of vaccine hesitancy in promoting public health campaigns.
  • Biomass: Sustainable solution development through the use of waste biomass for renewable energy production.

Adding to the significance of the event was the presence of the keynote speaker, renowned expert Dr. Omar M. Medany, in the pharmaceutical field. He shared his insights into the future of pharmacy and gave very useful advice to the participating students: emphasis on innovation, community service, and continuous learning in the pharmaceutical sciences.

ESPSA 2024 has given them an opportunity to exhibit exceptional talent, while underlining very strongly that the role of community outreach and collaborative effort in promoting healthcare and scientific research has been paramount. Competitions like ESPSA will serve to fill in the gap between academia and clinical or professional practice by promoting an attitude of excellence and service for the future professional in pharmacy.

Congratulations to all winners and participants for the remarkable contribution and engagement in bringing change for the betterment of pharmacists.
